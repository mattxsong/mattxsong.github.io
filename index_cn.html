Biography

I am now full-time working as a Research Assistant at Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences(SIAT), advised by Asso. Prof. Ruxin Wang . Before that, I received my Master's Degree from Beijing University of Technology(BJUT) in 2023, advised by Asso. Prof. Xiaodan Zhang and co-advised by Prof. Junzhong Ji.

My research interests include the combination of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Causality. I also possess an interest in Trustworthy and Factual Large Vision/Language Models for Biomedicine tasks.
