Biography

I am now a 3rd-year PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Kwang-Ting (Tim) Cheng and Prof. Hao Chen. Before that, I was a research engineer in Jarvis Lab at Tencent directed by Dr. Yefeng Zheng. I received my M.E. and B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2020 and 2016 respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Xin Yang. I also served as a volunteer teacher at Laifeng Middle School Attached to CCNU in 2016-2017.

My research interests include medical image analysis and deep learning. Recently, I focus on: 1) Label-efficient learning algorithms with human-in-the-loop; 2) Computer-aided-diagnosis systems (CADX) considering efficiency, scalability, and accessibility.
